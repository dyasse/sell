Nour el Quran - Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 11, 2026. This Privacy Policy explains how Nour el Quran collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect data necessary to provide core app features and improve app performance.

  • Location Data: Used to provide accurate prayer times and determine Qibla direction.
  • Storage Access: Used to download Quran audio files and play them offline on your device.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To enable location-based Islamic features (prayer times and Qibla).
  • To allow offline access to downloaded Quran recitations.
  • To monitor app stability, usage trends, and improve user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

Nour el Quran may use trusted third-party tools that process limited data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services (app functionality and security).
  • Firebase Analytics (anonymous usage analytics).
  • AdMob (ads delivery and performance measurement).

These services may collect device identifiers, diagnostic data, and interaction metrics as required to operate their services.

4. Children's Privacy

Nour el Quran does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

5. Data Deletion Requests

You may request deletion of data associated with your app use by contacting us at the support email below. In your request, please include enough information for us to identify your issue (for example, device type and app version). We will review and process valid requests within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
