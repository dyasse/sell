Privacy Policy

Nour Quran Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 21, 2026. Last updated: May 21, 2026.

Developer contact: support@nour-quran.com

Android package: com.dyasse.nourquran

Nour Quran does not sell personal or sensitive user data.

Data We Collect Or Process

  • Location: used only when you open prayer times or qibla features and choose to allow location. The Android app requests approximate location only. Browser implementations may provide coordinates through the web geolocation API. Location is sent to Aladhan for prayer times/qibla and BigDataCloud for city display.
  • Account data: if login/signup is enabled, Supabase processes your email address, user ID, authentication session, OAuth provider data, and password reset/sign-in metadata.
  • Local app data: preferences, bookmarks, last Quran reading position, audio resume position, theme, prayer offset, support popup state, and ad cooldown/page-view counters may be stored in localStorage or Capacitor Preferences on your device.
  • Ads and analytics: Google advertising services may process device identifiers, advertising IDs, approximate location inferred by Google, ad interactions, and diagnostics. Google Analytics and Vercel Analytics are loaded only when configured and may process page/app activity and device/browser data.
  • Quran services: Quran.com API, alquran.cloud, QuranicAudio, mp3quran.net, EveryAyah, and Islamic Network CDN may receive network request data such as IP address, requested chapter/ayah/audio file, device/browser metadata, and timestamps.
  • Support or donations: if you email support or use a donation/support provider such as PayPal, that provider and Nour Quran may process the information you submit for support, payment, fraud prevention, and record keeping.

How We Use Data

  • Calculate prayer times and qibla direction after your explicit action.
  • Provide Quran reading, tafsir, audio streaming, bookmarks, and resume features.
  • Operate account login/signup and password reset when configured.
  • Show ads, limit ad frequency, measure app performance, and improve reliability.
  • Respond to support, privacy, and account deletion requests.

Sharing With Third Parties

Data may be shared with service providers needed to operate the app: Supabase, Google advertising services, Google Analytics, Vercel Analytics, Aladhan, BigDataCloud, Quran.com API, alquran.cloud, QuranicAudio/audio CDNs, PayPal or other support providers, and hosting/CDN providers. Each provider processes data under its own terms and privacy policy.

Security And Retention

The app uses HTTPS for network requests where supported. Local device data remains on your device unless a feature sends it to a provider. Account data is retained while your account exists. Support, security, fraud-prevention, payment, and legal records may be retained as required. You can clear local app data by clearing browser/app storage.

Account And Data Deletion

If account creation is enabled, you can request deletion from Delete Account / Request Data Deletion or by emailing support@nour-quran.com. We target completion within 30 days after verifying the account owner. Some records may be retained where legally required or needed for security, fraud prevention, or financial records.

Children

Nour Quran is a general audience religious utility and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents or guardians can contact us to request review or deletion.

Content Sources

Quran text, metadata, tafsir, and audio may come from third-party providers. Current license status is documented on Content Sources & Licenses. Some provider permissions remain unverified and are release blockers for production.
