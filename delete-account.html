Account Deletion

Delete Account / Request Data Deletion

App: Nour Quran. Android package: com.dyasse.nourquran.

How To Request Deletion

Email support@nour-quran.com from the email address used for your Nour Quran account. Include “Nour Quran account deletion request” in the subject.

If you cannot email from the account address, include enough information for us to verify ownership before deletion.

What Is Deleted

  • Supabase authentication account, email, user ID, and active sessions where account creation is enabled.
  • Server-side profile/account records created by Nour Quran, if any exist.
  • Support data tied only to the deletion request when no longer needed.

What May Be Retained

We may retain records required for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, payment/donation records, abuse investigations, or backup integrity. Local device data such as bookmarks and preferences can be deleted by clearing app/browser storage on your device.

Processing Time

We target completion within 30 days after verifying the account owner. We will reply if more information is needed.
